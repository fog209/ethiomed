## Full Description
WardReady is an offline-first medical education app designed specifically for Ethiopian health science students preparing for the EHPLE examination.

### The 5 Pillars of WardReady
1. **Article-First Learning**: 10-section clinical articles with Ethiopian context
2. **Closed-Loop Quiz**: Questions linked to articles; wrong answers drive targeted review
3. **Spaced Repetition**: SM-2 algorithm schedules reviews based on your performance
4. **Learning Radar**: Visual heatmap shows your weak knowledge areas at a glance
5. **Past Exam Tags**: High-yield tags identify content tested in previous EHPLE exams

### Offline-First Design
- Sync once, study anywhere—even without internet
- All content stored locally in encrypted database
- Works on Ethiopian networks with intermittent connectivity

### Key Features
- 19 medical specialties with nested taxonomy
- Question review with quality ratings (1-5 scale)
- High-Yield mode hides low-yield content
- Streak tracking motivates daily study
- Bookmark favorite articles
- Clinical cases for interactive learning
