Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

WardReady collects the following data for educational purposes:

  • Study Progress: Number of articles read, quiz scores, and session timestamps (stored locally on device)
  • Quiz Performance: Question ratings and review history for spaced repetition scheduling
  • Device Information: App version and crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics when enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To track your study progress and maintain your personal learning streak
  • To personalize quiz scheduling using the SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm
  • To identify weak knowledge areas and prioritize review
  • To improve app stability through anonymous crash reporting

3. Data Security

All user data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. When you sync content from Supabase:

  • The anon role has SELECT-only access to content tables
  • No personal user data is transmitted to Supabase
  • Row Level Security policies prevent data modification by the anon role

4. Firebase Crashlytics

When Firebase is configured, we collect anonymous crash reports to fix bugs:

  • Stack traces and device state at time of crash
  • No personal health information is included in crash reports
  • You can disable crash reporting by uninstalling the app
